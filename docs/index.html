about the course

This portfolio contains the notes and the activities carried out in the series of bioinformatics courses.

The main purpose of the course series is to explore the fundamental ideas behind the main algorithms in bioinformatics. Each topic is developed presenting a logical line of thoughts from the problem definition to the actual solution proposal, passing through the series of ideas that came through along the way.

The attached notes are conformed by the materials given by the course authors as well as personal annotations, summaries, and code.
